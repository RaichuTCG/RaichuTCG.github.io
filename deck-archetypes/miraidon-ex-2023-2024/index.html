Miraidon ex

Miraidon ex is a deck currently being played in the 2023-2024 format. This deck revolves around Miraidon ex from Scarlet and Violet Base Set.

Strategy

Making use of Miraidon ex's Tandem Unit that lets you search your deck for two basic electric type pokemon and putting them onto your bench. This lets you find cards such as Mareeps to evolve into Flaffys and attackers such as Raikou V, Raichu V, and other Miraidon ex.
